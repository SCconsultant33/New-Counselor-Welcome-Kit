Start Here

Welcome to the county support network.

The original welcome kit opens with a simple promise: new counselors should feel at home in Oakland County and know where to turn for connection, consultation, and relevant resources.

Field Note

You are joining a countywide counseling community.

Oakland Schools serves all 28 traditional school districts and many public school academies in the county. The guide should help counselors connect beyond their building or district.

Communication

The listserv and weekly updates are the main signal path.

Oakland Schools maintains a counselors listserv to support communication across the county. Weekly updates are delivered through Smore on Mondays, with a bi-weekly rhythm during the summer.
